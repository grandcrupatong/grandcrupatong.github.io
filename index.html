The Residence

A simple brief, executed well.

Grand Cru Patong is a small apartment building in Kathu, a few minutes from the centre of Patong. Each unit is a furnished one-bedroom with bed and mattress, wardrobe, dressing table, refrigerator, and an en-suite bathroom with hot water shower.

Our residents are a mix of expats settling in for the year, foreign guests staying a season, and locals who simply want a clean, quiet place near everything. We don't try to be a hotel. We try to be a good address.
